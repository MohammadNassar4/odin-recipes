Garlic Mushrooms

Recipe Description

These garlic mushrooms are easy to make by saut√©ing sliced mushrooms in butter with red wine. Delicious with grilled steaks or on top of toasted bread for a tasty appetizer.

Garlic Mushrooms Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 2 pounds sliced fresh mushrooms
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 cup red wine

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Add mushrooms and garlic.
  3. Cook and stir until mushrooms are a light golden brown.
  4. Reduce heat to low, and pour wine into the skillet.
  5. Simmer until wine has mostly evaporated.
  6. Serve immediately.
